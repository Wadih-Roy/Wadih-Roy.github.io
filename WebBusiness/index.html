    ~About~

    Since opening its doors in 2021, Nabet Traditional Food has provided an authentic Lebanese dining experience. Founded by a group of Lebanese students from Georgian College, the restaurant aims to bring the flavors and atmosphere of Lebanon to the heart of Barrie. With a team of experienced and well-trained staff, Nabet ensures that every meal is a journey back to the traditional dishes of Lebanon.
